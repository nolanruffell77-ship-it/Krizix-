Our History

A structure built on passion, cohesion, and the ambition to make a mark on the esports scene.

Krizix Esport is an associative structure competing in several games, created in 2026 in Brittany by its founder Josko.

Born from a passion for video games and competition, Krizix aims to develop its presence on the francophone esports scene while maintaining a strong identity based on respect, cohesion, mutual aid, and performance.

Our greatest strength lies in our community and our true family spirit. Whether you are a competitive player, creator, supporter, or simply an esports enthusiast, you have a place among us.

Join the adventure and proudly wear the Koala's colors! 🖤💚🤍
Our Values

What defines us

Respect
